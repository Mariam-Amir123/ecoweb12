Learn About Plastics

What are Plastics?

Plastics are synthetic materials made from polymers that can cause significant environmental issues. They can originate from various sources including the breakdown of larger plastic items.

Solutions

To combat plastic pollution, consider reducing plastic use, supporting policies that limit plastic production, and participating in clean-up initiatives.
